# Sound Games — Discovery Call Prep

**Date:** March 6, 2026
**Contact:** Co-founder, Sound Games (Seattle)
**Origin:** Responded to first r/godot Reddit post

---

## Call Goals

1. **Validate the pain** — confirm they're feeling the W4 Build shutdown and understand their current workflow
2. **Map their needs** — learn exactly what CI/CD features matter most to a working studio
3. **Explore beta fit** — gauge willingness to be an early design partner / beta tester
4. **Build the relationship** — this is a potential lighthouse customer, not a sales call

---

## Questions to Ask

### Their Studio
- What games are you shipping / working on right now?
- How big is the dev team? How many use Godot?
- What platforms do you target (Steam, consoles, mobile, web)?

### Their Pain
- Were you using W4 Build? What happens after the Dec 2025 shutdown?
- What does your current CI/CD pipeline look like? (GitHub Actions, manual, etc.)
- What's the most painful part of your build/deploy workflow today?
- How much time per week does your team spend on build/release stuff?

### Feature Priorities
- What would a dream CI/CD tool for Godot look like for you?
- How important is multi-platform export vs. build caching vs. team collaboration?
- Do you need artifact hosting / distribution, or just builds?
- Would you want build notifications (Slack, Discord, email)?

### Business
- What would you pay for a tool that solved this? (gauge pricing sensitivity)
- Would you be open to being a beta tester / design partner?
- Are there other studios in your network hitting the same problem?

---

## What to Listen For

| Signal | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| Specific workflow pain points | Drives MVP feature priority |
| "We'd pay $X for this" | Validates pricing tiers ($19/$49) |
| "Other studios need this too" | Referral pipeline + market size |
| Platform targets (console, mobile) | Informs export template priority |
| Team size & structure | Shapes Teams tier features |
| Current workaround complexity | Measures urgency — worse workaround = faster adoption |

---

## What to Share

- **Vision:** Purpose-built CI/CD for Godot — not Jenkins glue
- **Timeline:** MVP targeting Q2 2026 with core build pipeline + multi-platform export
- **Ask:** We'd love a design partner who helps shape the product — early access, direct feedback loop
- **Credibility:** Already have waitlist signups, building in public, deeply embedded in the Godot community

### Keep Brief
- Don't oversell features that aren't built yet
- Focus on listening (80% them, 20% you)
- Take notes on exact words they use — great for landing page copy later
